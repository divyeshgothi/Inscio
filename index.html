What is TK Dealer Cloud?

TK Dealer Cloud is the culmination of a multi-year transformation effort to combine Thermo King dealer leading practices, the NetSuite unified business management suite, a new customer engagement model and business optimization methods into a unified cloud solution specifically designed for Thermo King dealers.

TK Dealer Cloud is engineered to solve unique challenges that historically have limited a dealer’s ability to grow, scale, adapt to change. Most dealer management vendors have only attempted to address a portion of a Thermo King dealer’s basic transactional needs and have been unable develop and deliver tools that help dealers execute on vision.

We’ve taken a holistic approach to Thermo King dealer needs and productized domain knowledge, leading practices, KPIs and an agile approach to product adoption. The benefits of this are faster time to value, increased business efficiency, flexibility and greater dealer success.

TK Dealer Cloud Overview

BUILD

A complete suite to support TK Dealers including service, rentals, equipment, parts, accounting,CRM, HR, ecommerce, and Business Intelligence (BI) built on the NetSuite cloud platform, continually updated with leading edge capabilities and technologies to support all of our industries.

ENGAGE

Leading dealer practices with built in roles including workflows, KPI, reports, and dashboards incorporating VSA/Dealer 2020 metrics like Dwell Time, First Time Fix, ime to Diagnose, Customer Satisfaction and more.

CONSUME

Intelligent staged approach via the TK Dealer Cloud allows dealers to take advantage of capabilities based on current needs and make iterative progress toward long term goals. A reimagined consumption model drives faster time to value, better ROI and greater user adoption.

OPTIMIZE

Dealers will benefit from continuous engagement, updated leading practices and new feature releases targeted to TK dealer iniatives. Dealers are always on the latest release with the flexibility to personalize on the TK Dealer Cloud platform.
